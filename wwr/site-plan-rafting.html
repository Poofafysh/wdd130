Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide an online presence for our rafting adventure company. It will showcase our services, provide information about trips, and allow potential customers to contact us and book adventures.

Audience

The target audience for this website includes adventure seekers, outdoor enthusiasts, and families looking for exciting outdoor activities. Scenario questions:

Branding

Are you looking for an adventurous outdoor activity to enjoy with friends or family?

Do you want to explore new and thrilling experiences in nature?

Are you interested in booking a rafting trip for your upcoming vacation?
